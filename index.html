WELCOME TO
BLUE FOX STUDIOS

We tell stories. And we burrow down to find the best way to tell them. We look to create experiences and widen the imagination so that the eyes can hear and the ears can see.

Blue Fox Studios is a creative post-production studio based in London and Dubai, providing editing, colour grading, post-sound and post-production supervising for online, broadcast and cinema content. We work across various genres, using our experience, technical know-how and industry-standard facilities to creatively push your project further. And further.

We strive to make post production accessible to everyone while also creating a safe space for LGBT+ and QTPOC individuals.
